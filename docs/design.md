# Design Notes

## Game Vision
**Yeet Party** is a chaotic local multiplayer brawler where physics, timing, and improvisation lead to unpredictable fun.  
The core philosophy: *make every throw count.*

---

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. Pick a character  
2. Yeet objects (and friends) around the arena  
3. Score points or survive longer than others  
4. New rounds begin with randomized modifiers

---

## Player Mechanics
- Move: analog stick / WASD  
- Jump: single / double jump  
- Grab / Throw: dynamic strength based on distance  
- Power-ups: temporary buffs or abilities  
- Hazards: environmental traps and breakable floors

---

## Art & Aesthetic
- Simple, colorful, minimal 3D or 2.5D art  
- Readable silhouettes and bright contrast  
- Smooth, punchy animation and screen feedback  

---

## Technical Overview
- Engine: **Godot 4.5**
- Language: **GDScript**
- Input: Local multiplayer (gamepads + keyboard) ???
- Physics: Built-in Godot Physics 3D or 2D (Jolt)

- Additional maps / hazards
